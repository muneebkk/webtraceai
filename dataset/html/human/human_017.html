Downing is a leading UK property investment and development company. We are known for transforming city centre sites into vibrant living and working spaces. With expertise in student accommodation, residential, commercial and educational buildings, our experienced team handles every aspect of project development and management.

With a 35+ year track record of positive community and environmental impact, we pride ourselves on creating thriving communities.
