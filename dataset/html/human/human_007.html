Surroundings

Monaco

Just 30 minutes from our villas, the Principality of Monaco is highly sought-after for its prestigious establishments and events.

Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat

This ancient fishing village is just fifteen minutes from our villas, and offers authentic corners full of charm.

Cannes

The town has become legendary for hosting some of the greatest names in the 7th art, and it’s only a 50-minute drive to the narrow streets of Cannes.

Saint-Tropez

This coastal town, famous for its beaches and nightlife, is a 2-hour drive from the villas, and was once a haven for artists and the 1960s jet set.
